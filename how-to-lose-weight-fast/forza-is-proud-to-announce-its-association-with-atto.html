Forza is proud to announce its association with ATTO.

Posted on November 22, 2011 by N Giannini There have been 0 comments

The Italian brand, dedicated to personal accessory design is the brain child of successful designer and entrepreneur Andrea Porcu. ATTO has received mentions in much of Italy’s press. Most Brits know that fashion houses are synonymous with 2 locations, Italy & France. In this case the Italian style house is based in Umbria, where the designers seek inspiration from the typical Italian lifestyle of fine wines, great food, warm climate and friendly gatherings where style is just a natural bi-product of life. Using these quintessential inspirations ATTO have been able to develop a range of designer bags, luggage & accessories.

“We believe in creating a product that can live out of trends, based on high quality materials, design with care and attention to the finer details; a product that will be a new classic. All of our designs are a result of a high level of artisanal handcraft, conjoined to an in-depth research of materials and appealing shapes. These factors give life to our collections and represent a work full of passion”, Andrea Porcu, designer and founder, ATTO

The ATTO brand is now becoming established in the Italian market and its directors are looking to expand its fan base to the UK through sourcing distributors that are looking to benefit from association to a high level Italian fashion brand and also from substantial financial benefits. To stake your claim on a territory in the UK please contact the company here: http://www.attobags.com/contacts

The UK accessories market has proven resilient in recession, adding nearly £0.5bn to its total value from 2005-2009. Clothing specialists, such as Marks & Spencer the market leader, use accessories as a vehicle for growth. Quoted as increasing at 14.4% between 2009 and 2013 by leading UK research websites the market is still an area to capitalise upon.
